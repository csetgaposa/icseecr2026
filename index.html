Welcome from the Organising Committee

Dear Colleagues and Researchers,

We are delighted to announce the International Conference on Sustainable Engineering, Environmental Innovation & Climate Resilience (ICSEECR 2026), hosted by Gateway ICT Polytechnic, Saapade, Ogun State, Nigeria.

ICSEECR 2026 provides a premier platform for interdisciplinary collaboration, knowledge exchange, and promoting innovative research in sustainable engineering, environmental innovation, and climate resilience. The conference brings together researchers, engineers, policy makers, and industry professionals from across the globe to address the most pressing challenges of our time.

We warmly invite all researchers and professionals to participate and share their expertise across our two thematic schools — Engineering & Technology and Environmental Studies — covering smart grids, renewable energy, AI-driven environmental monitoring, climate adaptation, waste management, and much more.

All accepted and presented papers will be considered for publication in the IEEE Xplore Digital Library (subject to IEEE approval), following a rigorous double-blind peer-review process.

We look forward to your participation in ICSEECR 2026 — a global forum for innovation, sustainability, and environmental resilience.

Best regards,
ICSEECR 2026 Organizing Committee
Gateway ICT Polytechnic, Saapade, Ogun State, Nigeria
